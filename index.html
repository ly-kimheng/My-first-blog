The Scent of Nostalgia

      A scent has its own belonging, sometimes it can be a person, a place or it can be your favorite home-cooked meals. Yet, it’s funny how the scents can unexpectedly bring you back to the past, unlocking the memory of your childhood you hadn’t thought of in years, Sometimes those sudden wave of emotion can be triggered by something as simple as a stranger’s perfume, the smells of rain or foods as you walk by.

      The pictures above are from the korean drama which the title is called "Welcome to Samdal-ri" . In the middle picture, It shows the character is named Cho Sam-dal where she was waiting for the train but suddenly, It seemed like she paralyzed in one place while her surroundings continue to move forward.This powerful emotional shift was caused by the scent of the stranger’s perfume, which holds her back in a moment.Her reaction wasn't just a physical pause that stop her from moving but it's a triggered for her past, which involed her ex-boyfriend who was her first love.
